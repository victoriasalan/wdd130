Ecuador

Ecuador is a multicultural and megadiverse country. It is full of a great variety of flora and fauna. Ecuador has many geographical areas, such as the Andean peaks, tropical rainforests and, 600 miles from the coast, the volcanic Galapagos Islands, home to animals and birds whose evolutionary adaptations shaped Charles Darwin's theories.
